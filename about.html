Who Are We?

Muscle &: Hate is a collective focused on producing and sharing creative music content. We are a record label with the aim of distributing music that spiritually embodies outsider culture. Our brand coincides with various mediums eg. zines, interviews, writing, exhibitions and club nights: creating collaborative and expressive platforms in a celebration of otherness...
